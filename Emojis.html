    What is Emotion Detection and how does it work?

    Emotion detection is form of machine learning and works by training a computer model based off a dataset of keywords and phrases that relate to a certain emotion. From this information, when the model is shown text the algorithm chooses the emotion it believes that the string of text conveys.


    What are datasets?

    Datasets are groups of words that have been tagged or annotated with the emotion that corresponds to each specific word or phrase. The AI model uses these datasets to learn and become more accurate when detecting emotion.

    How does my Emoshie change emotion?

    You can connect your twitter to your Emoshie via our website and your Emoshie’s emotion will change based on what you tweet. You can also write something personal into the website interface and your Emoshie will convey that emotion too. We will not store anything that you write on our website. There will also be options to pause on a certain emotion which will prevent your Emoshie from changing emotions.
